How To Download & Install The Map

1. Click on the DOWNLOAD NOW button

2. Extract the map file you have just downloaded.

3. Press Window+R to open the Run command window, type %appdata% then press Enter to open The Roaming folder.

4. Inside the .minecraft folder, you should see a folder named saves, place the extracted map file into this folder.

5. Open the game and click on Singleplayer. Choose the world you have just installed and click Play selected world to start your adventure.


RULES

Resource Pack is REQUIRED

Gamemode must stay ADVENTURE

DO NOT breaking or placing blocks

You must be 18 YEARS OLD or OLDER to experience the map

It is FORBIDDEN to cheat

Best played with headphones

Using OPTIFINE HD G8 Ultra can help improve FPS (frames per second) performance

Play in SINGLEPLAYER

DO NOT use any other perspective than the first person perspective

If you want to use shaders, SEUS Shaders by Sonic Ether is a suitable choice

These rules will make a better gameplay experience!
