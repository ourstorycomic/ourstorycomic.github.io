What is RW Studio?

rw-studio is a small studio built by 3 people in april 2022, we make some cool minecraft comics and tv shows, give you the most enjoyable experience of browsing through our products.

How can i comment?

To comment you need to register an account at disqus.com, after registering you just have to go back in and scroll down to the comment section it will allow you to comment.

How can I watch tv series and read the comics?

To watch our tv series, you just have to go to tv shows and find the movie you want to watch. To read our comics, you just have to go to comics and find the comics you want to read, btw don't forget to leave a comment under.

Does RW Studio support other languages on TV series and comics?

For now we only use English for our comics, for TV shows we use multiple languages like German, Spanish, Vietnamese, Italian and more If you want the language of your country in the movie, you can contact us at discord to become translators, maybe in the future we will use other languages for our comics.
